Rules and Regulations

Absence

If any reason occurs for the students to stay absent, they must take permission earlier. In sudden cases too, they have to apply to the principal by showing the real fact with the guardian's recommendation.

If any student stays absent without permission, S/he will be fined Tk. 20.00 per day. If a student stays absent for more than five (05) days, the guardians must be present to show cause. Staying absent for three (03) or more than three days due to illness will be considered sympathetically, but an application with a medical certificate has to be submitted in this regard.

Playing truant is a crime. So, a student will be fined Tk. 100.00 for playing truant.

Guardian’s Day

On Guardian’s Day, it is discussed with guardians about their wards’ academic progress and promotion of results. In this way, they can share their opinions, ask questions, get information, and provide suggestions as well.

Besides, guardians are regularly informed of the important information and rules of the institution.

Rules for Paying Monthly Fees

  • Monthly fees must be paid from the 5th to the 20th of the month. If the final date is a public holiday, fees must be paid on the next working day.
  • If anyone is unable to pay monthly fees in due time and wants to pay later, they will have to pay Tk. 50.00 as a fine.
  • If any student fails to pay the fees for three consecutive months, they will be unrolled from the register book. Later, by paying Tk. 300.00 as a fine and with the Principal’s permission, their name may be re-enrolled in the register book.
  • The bank responsible for the collection of money is Dhaka Bank Ltd.
